# NexaMind Enterprise Policies

## Vacation and Paid Time Off (PTO)
Employees at NexaMind are entitled to 25 days of paid time off (PTO) per calendar year. Vacation requests must be submitted through the internal HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance of the planned absence. A maximum of 5 unused PTO days can be rolled over to the next calendar year.

## Remote Work Policy
NexaMind supports a hybrid work environment. Employees are expected to work from the corporate office at least 3 days per week (Tuesday through Thursday). Mondays and Fridays are optional remote work days, subject to manager approval and team alignment.

## Expense Reimbursement
All business expenses exceeding $50 must be pre-approved by the department head. Receipts must be uploaded to the Finance Portal within 30 days of the transaction. Reimbursements are processed on the 15th and 30th of each month.
